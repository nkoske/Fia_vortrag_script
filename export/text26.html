RAID 0: Fs1 + Fs2 = FsGes

500 GB + 500 GB = 1000 GB = 1 TB

Fs= Festplattenspeicher

RAID 1: Fs1 , Fs2 = FsGes

500GB, 500GB = 500GB

RAID 5: (FAnzahl – 1) * Fs = FsGes

(3 – 1) * 500 GB = 1000 GB = 1,0 TB

RAID 6:

(FAnzahl – 2) * Fs = FsGes

(4 – 2) * 500 GB = 1000 GB = 1 TB

RAID 10:

(Fanz / 2) * Fs = FsGes

(4 / 2) * 500 GB = 1000 GB = 1 TB
